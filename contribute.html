How to Contribute

Cryptonoia is a collaborative project focused on the Solana blockchain ecosystem. We welcome contributions from everyone, whether you're a developer, content creator, designer, or simply someone enthusiastic about cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. Here's how you can contribute:

  • Developers: If you're interested in contributing code, please check our GitHub for open issues or suggest new features.
  • Designers: We always need creative minds to help improve our UI/UX. If you have design ideas or skills, reach out!
  • Content Creators: If writing or creating educational content is more your style, we'd love to hear your ideas on topics to cover.
  • Everyone: Any form of support, whether it's spreading the word, providing feedback, or donating, is greatly appreciated.

Contact Information

Ready to contribute or have questions? Please contact us directly at crypthon777@proton.me, or submit an issue or pull request on our GitHub repository.
